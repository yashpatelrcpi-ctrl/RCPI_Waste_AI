❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I file a complaint?
Go to the Complaints page and fill out the form with your details. Our team will review your complaint and respond within 24 hours.
How can I track waste collection in my area?
Visit the Waste Collection page to see all collections in your ward. You can also check the Dashboard for overall collection statistics.
Where are the waste collection vehicles?
Check the Vehicles page to see all active waste collection vehicles, their assigned wards, and driver information.
How do I add a new ward?
Go to the Wards page and click "Add New Ward". Fill in the ward details including name, area, email, and address.
How can I view waste collection reports?
Visit the Reports page to see detailed analytics about complaints, waste collection by type, and other statistics with charts.
What are the different waste types?
We categorize waste into: Organic, Recyclable, Mixed, Electronic, and Construction waste. Each type is handled differently.
How often is waste collected?
Waste collection frequency depends on your ward. Check with your ward administrator or the Waste Collection page for your area's schedule.
Who should I contact for emergencies?
For emergencies, file a complaint through the Complaints page marked as urgent, or contact your ward administrator directly using the contact information in the Wards page.
